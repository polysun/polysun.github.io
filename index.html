Poly Z.H. Sun is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Industrial Intelligent System with the Department of Industrial Engineering, School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China.

Dr. Sun has authored/co-authored over 10 research papers in refereed international journals and conferences. His current research interests include evolutionary optimization, neuroergonomics, brain-inspired intelligence, cognitive computing, complex network, non-parametric machine learning, operations research, knowledge automation, and their applications in industrial or medical problems. He is a member of the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society and Association for Computing Machinery. He also has been severing as the reviewer for many top-tier international journals and conferences in his research field.

In addition, Dr. Sun has promoted the standardization of Industrial Internet and Industrial IoT. The standards he participated in promoted the new paradigm of manufacturing networking in China.

Since 2021, his research has shifted from EAs-based Operational Research to

  • Intelligent Transportation Systems: Robustness and Connectivity Analysis of Air Transport Networks under Uncertain Factors, Emission Policies and Monitoring Technologies of Offshore Vessels
  • Brain-Computers Interface and Cogitive computing: Pilot Driving Cognition, Neuroergonomics
  • Medical Diagnosis: Depression, Autism
