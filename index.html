Do not go gentle into that good night— Dylan Thomas

Poly Z.H. Sun is currently pursuing his Ph.D. degree in Industrial Intelligent System with the Department of Industrial Engineering, School of Mechanical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China.

He has authored/co-authored over 20 research papers in refereed international journals and conferences. His current research interests include neuroergonomics, brain science, brain-inspired intelligence, cognitive computing, complex network, non-parametric machine learning, operations research, evolutionary optimization, knowledge automation, and their applications in industrial or medical problems. He is a member of the IEEE CIS, IEEE SMC, and Association for Computing Machinery. He also has been severing as the reviewer for many top-tier international journals and conferences in his research field.

In addition, he participated the standardization of Industrial Internet Identification and Resolution. The standard he participated is promoting the development of manufacturing networking in China.

Since 2021, his research has shifted from Evolutionary Algorithms-based Operational Research to

  • Brain and Cognitive Science: Functional Brain Segmentation, Brain Activation Modeling, and Cognitive Analysis, Neural Circuit of Attention, Causal Relationship Between Brain Regions and Behavior, Pilot Driving Cognition, Neuroergonomics
  • Intelligent Transportation Systems: Human factors and ergonomics in the context of Transportation, Man-machine co-driving, Sustainability issues in transportation systems
  • Medical Diagnosis: Depression, Autism, Insomnia, Alzheimer's disease
