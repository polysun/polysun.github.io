Do not go gentle into that good night— Dylan Thomas

Poly Z.H. Sun received the Ph.D. degree in Industrial Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Shanghai, China. He is currently an Associate Professor with the Department of Artificial Intelligence and Human Factors Engineering, School of Psychology and Cognitive Science, and the Director of Personal Informatics System Lab (PIS-Lab) at East China Normal University, Shanghai, China.

Prof. Sun has authored/co-authored over 50 research papers (including 30+ IEEE Trans. papers) in top-tier refereed international journals and conferences, such as IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Transportation Systems, IEEE Transactions on Industrial Informatics, IEEE Transactions on Computational Social Systems, IEEE Transactions on Intelligent Vehicles, IEEE Transactions on Engineering Management, IEEE Transactions on Emerging Topics in Computational Intelligence, IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering, IEEE/ACM Transactions on Computational Biology and Bioinformatics, IEEE Transactions on Cognitive and Developmental Systems, The Innovation (Cell Press), Engineering, IEEE Journal of Biomedical and Health Informatics, IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters.

His current research interests include human-computer interaction, computer-supported cooperative work, social computing, digital healthcare, intelligent transportation system, and human factors in driving. Dr. Sun is currently an Associate Editor of IEEE Journal of Radio Frequency Identification. He also has been serving as a reviewer for 30+ top-tier refereed international journals and conferences, an Associate Chair of ACM SIGCHI Conversational User Interfaces (CUI) 2026, ACM CHI conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI) 2026, ACM SIGCHI Conference on Automotive User Interfaces and Interactive Vehicular Applications (AutomotiveUI) 2024-2025, ACM Conference on Computer-Supported Cooperative Work and Social Computing (CSCW) 2024-2026, ACM International Conference on Interactive Media Experiences (IMX) 2025-2026, an Area Chair of International Joint Conference on Neural Networks (IJCNN) 2025-2026, and a session chair for several conferences such as IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC) 2022, International Conference on Intelligent Robotics and Applications (ICIRA) 2022, IEEE International Conference on Industrial Engineering and Engineering Management (IEEM) 2021.
